What we do at Helixionn Innovations ?


The development of biotechnology research and innovations is a promising strategy to address global issues pertaining to health, agriculture and industry. Sri Lanka, being a rich pool of biodiversity and a strong hub for biotechnological knowledge, however lacks the drive towards research, development, innovation and entrepreneurship. At Helixionn Innovations, we are driven towards identifying this scientific niche, prioritizing the requirement of bridging the research barrier with the global market and a iding research, innovations and entrepreneurship in Sri Lanka. Helixionn Innovations provides customized scientific strategies, conceptual designs, pilot scale processes and tools, and industrial scale models and equipment to meet the research and innovation demands of its customers.
