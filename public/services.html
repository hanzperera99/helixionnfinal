Scientific Innovation strategies

We develop scientific innovation strategies based on biotechnological modifications and automation systems. The concepts are developed based on the customer requirement and we provide strategies and solutions to enhance the efficiency, accuracy and the cost effectiveness of the product or the process.



Research and development

The Research and development team at Helixionn Innovations is a continuously functioning body that address the niches of the developing scientific world. We engage in conceptualization of innovative strategies through biotechnology and automation that leads to commercially valuable product development.




Consultation

We provide scientific innovation consultation to upcoming entrepreneurs and young scientists in the fields of life sciences, plant tissue culture, hydroponics, bioreactor systems, and automation.




In silico and functional pilot scale innovation models

We at Helixionn Innovations also facilitate designing of pilot scale models both in silico and functional scale to ease the customer requirement. These designs are ensured of its accuracy and efficiency through guaranteed quality analyses.
