Semi Automated Combined Flat Pan for Palm Syrup Processing
The first of its kind in Sri Lanka that is designed to ease the processing of Palm Syrup, the traditional “kithul treacle”. The designed system is a locally developed semi-automated combined flat pan; a smart choice to be used by both large scale and medium scale producers of kithul treacle to increase the efficiency of production while maintaining the quality of the treacle.

Key features Product Specifications

  • Semi – automated.

  • User friendly.

  • Guaranteed warranty.

  • Increased efficiency.

  • Rapid technology.

  • Improved flavor induction.

  • Low cost of maintenance.

  • No depletion of reducing sugar content in the final mixture.

  • Temperature monitoring with thermal regulators.

  • Enhanced end product quality.
  • Oil Jacketed Flat pan with burn oil manufactured with Stainless steel; Inner SS-316L (food grade) and Outer (for direct flame) SS-304.

  • Capacity - 200L.

  • Specially designed agitator SS-316L for high viscous fluids.

  • Motor (01HP-400W) driven agitator with Speed Controlling Unit,

  • High grade LP gas burner with furnace.

  • Flue Gas Exhauster.

  • Brick furnace for firewood with smoke influx system.

  • Safety insulation system for burner, furnace and other required surfaces.

  • Safety valves for burner oil jacket.

  • Thermal probe with high accuracy.

  • Gas Flow Meter.
Temporary Immersion Bioreactor System for Plant Propagation
A system to speed up the process of plant propagation. An all-in-one liquid based bioreactor system that ensures optimum conditions for speedy plant growth and propagation. Highly recommended for mass scale production of exotic, ornamental and medicinal plant propagation. Guaranteed production of xxxx plants per annum that are disease free and of high quality. Key features

  • Use of liquid nutrient media.

  • Variable size.

  • Suited for all environmental conditions.

  • Single dose nutrient media system.

  • Complete growth of the plants in a single system.

  • Automated nutrient flow system.

  • Automated air flow system.

  • Oxygen enrichment ability.

  • Activated Oxygen Technology to facilitate disinfection.

  • Microfiltration.

  • Reduced cost.

  • User friendly.
Ultrafiltration System
Ultrafiltration hollow fiber cartridges designed for pilot-scale cross flow filtration with a range of pore sizes and membrane areas. To bridge the several steps between research and production volumes, we offer a full range of pilot-scale ultrafiltration and microfiltration hollow fiber cartridge filters. Both 30 cm and 60 cm flow path lengths are offered with cartridges that provide an order-of-magnitude membrane area span from 0.12 to 1.15 m² (1.3 to 12.5 ft²).

Key features

  • Smooth scale-up: comprehensive range of cartridges facilitates intermediate scale-up steps between research and production volumes

  • Industry-standard design: 1.5-inch Tri-Clamp sanitary feed and retentate fittings.

  • Hollow fiber technology: minimizes macrovoids and pinhole defects for exceptional performance.

  • Cross flow filtration for intermediate scale-up steps.

  • Complete growth of the plants in a single system.
